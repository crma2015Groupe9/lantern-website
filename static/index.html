Before going to bed,

children are often afraid of leaving their parent’s side. Some tend to extend their bedtime as much as they can, usually by wanting to listen to another story, then another one, and this can go on for hours.

Our solution

We wanted to create a bedtime routine that combines two of the strongest existing solutions: the bedtime story and the nightlight.

Lantern

is a nightlight that creates an atmosphere of sound and light according to the story the parent reads.

Light

Elegant and subtle lights follow the story and can illustrate a specific moment thanks to LED animations.

Sound

The story is given life thanks to soft melodies and sounds that can also accompany the child’s sleep.

Stories

The child and the parent can choose among a large choice of bedtime classic stories with illustrated covers.
